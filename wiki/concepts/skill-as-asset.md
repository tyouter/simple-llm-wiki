**Skill as Asset** represents the fundamental conceptual shift at the heart of the [[Agent Skills Framework]]: moving from viewing AI interaction via disposable [[Prompt Engineering|prompts]] to building reusable, composable, and iteratively improvable Skill assets. A prompt is a one-time instruction, while a Skill is a durable, version-controlled asset that encapsulates standardized processes, organizational knowledge, tone, and tool usage. This mindset justifies the investment in [[Iterative Skill Development]] and tools like the [[Skill-Creator Tool]]. Skills can be shared, combined (following [[Modular & Focused Skill Design]] principles), and continuously optimized. The analysis posits this as the key productivity differentiator, turning personal and team knowledge into a scalable, leverageable system. The frontier thus becomes not 'can you write a prompt?' but 'can you productize your workflow into Skills?'
