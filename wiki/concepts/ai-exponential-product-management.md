# AI-Exponential Product Management

## Definition
A new product management paradigm where traditional assumptions about stable technical constraints are invalidated by exponentially improving AI models. This approach requires rapid experimentation, continuous delivery, and betting on effective directions rather than relying on long-term planning.

## Core Principles

### Exponential Model Improvement
Unlike traditional software where capabilities are relatively stable during development cycles, AI models like those from [[Anthropic]] improve at an exponential rate. This means that:
- Technical constraints that existed at project start may disappear mid-cycle
- Features that were impossible may become trivial with newer models
- Optimization strategies must account for rapidly changing capabilities

### Rapid Experimentation Over Planning
Instead of extensive upfront planning, this approach emphasizes:
- Running many small experiments to test hypotheses
- Using [[Edge Exploration Projects (Side Quests)]] for autonomous testing
- Making decisions based on empirical results rather than predictions

### Continuous Adaptation
Product teams must:
- Regularly re-evaluate features with each new model release
- Be willing to discard approaches made obsolete by new capabilities
- Maintain flexibility in implementation strategies

## Implementation at Anthropic
According to [[Cat Wu]], Anthropic has implemented this approach through:

### Tool-Based Workflow
- **[[Claude.ai]]** for strategy and problem-solving
- **[[Claude Code]]** for prototyping and development
- **[[Cowork]]** for administrative tasks

This represents a form of [[AI Tool Chaining/Combination]] optimized for product management.

### Organizational Shifts
1. **From certainty to discovery** - As noted by [[Kai Xin Tai]], the focus shifts from locking in certainty to accelerating discovery
2. **From documentation to demonstration** - Prioritizing [[Prototype-First Development]] over extensive documentation
3. **From complex to simple** - Following the [[Simplest Working Solution Principle]]
4. **From static to evolving** - Practicing [[Model-Capability-Driven Product Evolution]]

## Impact on Development
[[Bihan Jiang]] reports that this approach significantly increases the ceiling for high-quality product teams by shortening the path from idea to prototype.

## Relation to Other Concepts
- Contradicts traditional product management's assumption of stable technical constraints
- Challenges documentation-first approaches
- Questions the value of extensive engineering workarounds for model limitations
- Complements [[gstack-role-based-ai-development-workflow-for-claude-code]] though emphasizes more organic experimentation
