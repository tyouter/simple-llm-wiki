# 7-Step Strategic Planning Framework

## Definition
A practical strategic planning methodology consisting of seven sequential steps for developing and implementing enterprise strategy. This framework was shared by Zhihu user [[HAHAPPYPPY]] in the discussion [[Zhihu Discussion: Strategic Planning Methodologies and Implementation Challenges]].

## The Seven Steps

### 1. Analyze Current Market Environment
- Conduct comprehensive market research
- Analyze industry trends and competitive landscape
- Assess internal capabilities and resources
- Identify opportunities and threats

### 2. Define Strategic Positioning
- Determine unique value proposition
- Establish competitive differentiation
- Define target market segments
- Clarify business scope and boundaries

### 3. Analyze Gaps
- Perform [[Strategic Gap Analysis]] between current state and objectives
- Identify performance gaps (current vs. desired performance)
- Identify opportunity gaps (untapped market potential)
- Prioritize gaps based on impact and feasibility

### 4. Set and Decompose Goals
- Establish clear, measurable strategic objectives
- Break down goals into departmental and individual targets
- Implement [[Three-Level Sales Target System]] for performance management
- Align goals with resource allocation

### 5. Create Strategic Map
- Visualize strategic relationships and dependencies
- Map cause-effect relationships between objectives
- Identify key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Create balanced scorecard framework

### 6. Execute Actions
- Develop detailed action plans
- Assign responsibilities and timelines
- Allocate resources effectively
- Establish monitoring and control mechanisms

### 7. Strategic Evaluation
- Regularly review progress against objectives
- Adjust strategies based on performance data
- Learn from implementation challenges
- Continuously improve planning process

## Practical Applications
- Suitable for small and medium-sized enterprises
- Provides structured approach to strategic planning
- Helps bridge the gap between strategy formulation and execution
- Addresses common implementation challenges in Chinese enterprises
