# Every

## Overview
[[Every]] is an organization that developed the 'Compound Engineering' methodology, a systematic approach to knowledge accumulation and reuse in software development. The organization has contributed significantly to the conceptual framework of AI-driven development practices.

## Key Contribution: Compound Engineering
Every's primary contribution is the development and promotion of [[Compound Engineering]] (复利工程), a methodology that emphasizes:

1. **Knowledge Accumulation**: Documenting plans, execution details, reviews, and lessons learned from every engineering task
2. **Reuse Systems**: Creating mechanisms for storing, organizing, and retrieving accumulated knowledge
3. **Process Integration**: Embedding knowledge management into standard development workflows
4. **Long-Term Value Creation**: Treating engineering work as an investment that generates "compound interest" for future projects

## Methodology Details

### Core Philosophy
Every's approach is based on the principle that engineering work should not only solve immediate problems but also create reusable assets that accelerate future development. This contrasts with approaches like [[Vibe Coding]] that prioritize short-term productivity gains.

### Implementation Framework
The compound engineering methodology includes:
- Structured documentation standards
- Knowledge base organization systems
- Retrieval and reuse mechanisms
- Process integration patterns

## Relationship to Other Concepts

### Harness Engineering
Every's [[Compound Engineering]] complements [[Harness Engineering]] by providing the knowledge management layer that makes AI-driven development sustainable. While harness engineering focuses on AI agent infrastructure and workflows, compound engineering ensures the knowledge generated during those workflows is preserved and leveraged.

### Industry Context
Every operates within the broader ecosystem of AI-driven development methodologies that includes approaches from [[OpenAI]], [[Anthropic]], and other organizations pioneering [[Agent-First Development]].

## Practical Applications

### Development Workflows
Every's methodology has been applied in real-world projects, demonstrating how systematic knowledge accumulation can accelerate development cycles and improve quality.

### AI Integration
The compound engineering approach is particularly relevant for AI-driven development, as it provides structured knowledge that AI agents can learn from and reuse.

## Significance in AI Development Landscape

Every represents an important voice in the evolution of software engineering practices for the AI era, emphasizing:

1. **Sustainable Development**: Moving beyond quick wins to build lasting value
2. **Knowledge-Centric Approaches**: Treating knowledge as a primary engineering asset
3. **Systematic Processes**: Creating repeatable, scalable methodologies for development

## Industry Influence

While specific implementation details of Every's work may not be as widely documented as larger organizations, their conceptual contributions to [[Compound Engineering]] have influenced thinking about:
- Knowledge management in AI-driven development
- Long-term value creation in engineering work
- Integration of learning and reuse into development processes

## Future Directions

As AI becomes more integrated into software development, methodologies like Every's compound engineering are likely to become increasingly important for:
- Managing the knowledge generated by AI agents
- Creating sustainable development practices
- Ensuring human oversight and understanding of AI-generated systems
