# 杜千

## Identity
A Zhihu user and content creator who runs the WeChat public account 「杜千」. The account focuses on interpreting public opinion phenomena and discussing history and culture from a analytical perspective.

## Contribution to the Discussion
In the [[Zhihu Discussion on Enlightenment and Personal Transformation]], 杜千 provided the primary **neuroscientific and cognitive framework** for understanding enlightenment.

## Key Arguments and Explanation
杜千's explanation centers on the [[Brain Prediction Model]]:
1. **The Brain as a Prediction Engine**: Argued that the brain's core function is not to understand, but to predict, constantly generating and updating a model of the world.
2. **Enlightenment as Model Updating**: Defined [[Enlightenment (开悟)]] as the conscious ability and tendency to update one's core cognitive models (a metabolically expensive process) in response to prediction errors, rather than taking the easier path of reinterpreting data to fit the old model.
3. **The Three-Stage Journey**: Articulated the process of cognitive development through the [[Three Stages of Understanding]] (看山是山，看山不是山，看山还是山), framing it as a journey of increasing model flexibility.

## Perspective and Tone
- **Scientific and Secular**: Demystifies enlightenment, stripping it of spiritual or mystical connotations and presenting it as a cognitive skill.
- **Mechanistic**: Focuses on the "how" and "why" of the process using concepts from neuroscience and cognitive psychology.
- **Analytical**: Breaks down a complex, abstract concept into a testable, logical framework.

## Relation to Other Contributors
- Provides the theoretical foundation that the practical advice of [[青峰哥]] and the behavioral descriptions of [[驷马奔腾]] can be seen to complement.
- His scientific perspective **contradicts** traditional religious or mystical interpretations of enlightenment.
