# China National Library (中国国家图书馆)

## Overview
The National Library of China is the largest comprehensive research library and national bibliographic center in China. It holds vast collections of ancient and modern texts, periodicals, and other materials.

## Digital Presence as a Knowledge Hub
Beyond its physical institution, the National Library's digital portal provides extensive online resources, including digitized ancient texts, academic databases, and public lectures. In the context of the [[Zhihu Discussion: Alternative Websites After Tianya Forum Closure]], its digital services are highlighted as a premier, authoritative [[Niche Knowledge & Creative Hubs|knowledge hub]] for serious research and learning.
