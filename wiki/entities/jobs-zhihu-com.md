# jobs@zhihu.com

## Definition
The official recruitment email address for Zhihu, the Chinese question-and-answer platform.

## Context and Function
This entity appears within the [[Zhihu Login Page Error Message (Source)]] as a visible point of contact for employment opportunities. Its inclusion on a public-facing page (even an error page) indicates that:
1.  **Integrated Recruitment**: Talent acquisition is a visible and integrated part of Zhihu's platform strategy and user experience.
2.  **Direct Channel**: It provides a direct, low-friction communication channel for potential candidates, bypassing more formal career portal structures.
3.  **Brand Alignment**: Positioning recruitment alongside core platform features (like content interaction benefits) suggests Zhihu views attracting talent as complementary to building its knowledge community.

## Significance
While seemingly a simple contact detail, its prominent placement underscores the strategic importance of human capital for a knowledge-centric platform like [[Zhihu]]. It reflects a blending of community, content, and company growth objectives.
