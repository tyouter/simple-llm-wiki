# Zhang Xue Motorcycle (张雪机车)

## Identity
The motorcycle manufacturing company founded by [[Zhang Xue]] after leaving his first company, [[Kaiyue]]. Known for championship-winning models and embodying [[Dream-First, Business-Second Entrepreneurship]].

## Founding Context
- Established after Zhang Xue left [[Kaiyue]], the first company he founded
- Created with support from mentor [[Zhang Jixing]]
- Located strategically in Chongqing to leverage [[Industrial Cluster Advantage]]

## Business Philosophy
Embodies [[Dream-First, Business-Second Entrepreneurship]]:
- Primary goal: Build world-championship-winning motorcycles
- Business structure serves the technical/engineering dream
- Commercial viability necessary but secondary to technical excellence

## Key Models

### ZX-500RR
Early model demonstrating technical capabilities and establishing brand reputation.

### 820RR
Flagship model that achieved world championship success.

## Competitive Achievements
- Won WSBK (World Superbike Championship) world championship in 2026
- Established technical credibility against established international brands
- Demonstrated that Chinese motorcycle manufacturers could compete at world championship level

## Investment History
Secured 90 million RMB investment from state-owned venture capital firm [[Zhechuangtou]], with investment decision driven significantly by confidence in founder [[Zhang Xue]]'s character and technical能力.

## Strategic Advantages

### Location Strategy
Based in Chongqing, China's motorcycle manufacturing hub, leveraging [[Industrial Cluster Advantage]] for supply chain efficiency and talent access.

### Founder-Driven Innovation
[[Zhang Xue]]'s hands-on engineering background and racing insight drove product development focused on real performance needs.

### Authenticity Narrative
Company story built on genuine underdog journey rather than manufactured marketing, contributing to [[Authenticity as Capital]].

## Industry Significance
- Represents successful implementation of [[Strategic Pivot from Racing to Manufacturing]]
- Demonstrates viability of passion-driven manufacturing in China
- Challenges perception that Chinese manufacturing focuses only on cost, not quality/innovation
