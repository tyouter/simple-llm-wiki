## Role in Discussion
Primary answerer in the [[Zhihu Discussion: Comparative Analysis of Foreign vs. Domestic Textbooks]] who provides a detailed, resource-rich argument praising the self-study design of American textbooks.

1. **Central Argument**: Advocates strongly for the [[Self-Study Friendly Textbook Design]] of major American textbooks, contrasting them favorably with many contemporary domestic options.
2. **Resource Curation**: Acts as a guide to [[Open Educational Resources (OER)]] by compiling and sharing links to:
   - **University Open Courses**: Materials from Harvard, Stanford, Carnegie Mellon.
   - **Open Textbook Libraries**.
   - **Online Learning Platforms**: Specifically highlights [[Khan Academy (可汗学院)]] for humanities subjects.
3. **Publisher Endorsement**: Cites [[HMH (Houghton Mifflin Harcourt), Mcgraw Hill, Pearson]] as producers of high-quality, reliable textbooks.

## Implied Perspective
This user values education that is accessible, structured for independent learning, and supported by abundant, free, high-quality resources. Their answer is practical and aimed at empowering self-learners.
