# Steph Ango (kepano)

## Overview
The CEO of [[Obsidian]] and developer of the [[obsidian-skills]] toolkit, which enables AI agents to generate Obsidian-style Markdown and structured content. Known by the username "kepano" in the Obsidian community.

## Contributions to Obsidian

### Leadership Role
- **Company Direction**: Guides Obsidian's development philosophy and feature priorities.
- **Community Engagement**: Active in community discussions and feedback collection.
- **Product Vision**: Shapes Obsidian's approach to knowledge management and note-taking.

### Technical Contributions
- **[[obsidian-skills]] Toolkit**: Created an open-source toolkit that allows AI agents to generate native Obsidian content.
- **Feature Development**: Involved in core Obsidian feature design and implementation.
- **API Design**: Contributes to Obsidian's plugin API and extensibility architecture.

## obsidian-skills Toolkit
- **Purpose**: Bridge between AI systems and Obsidian's content model.
- **Skills Included**: obsidian-markdown, json-canvas, obsidian-bases, and other specialized capabilities.
- **AI Integration**: Enables agents like Claude Code to create properly formatted Obsidian content.

## Philosophy and Approach
- **Human-Centric Design**: Focus on tools that augment rather than replace human thinking.
- **Open Standards**: Emphasis on open formats (Markdown) and interoperability.
- **Practical Innovation**: Developing features that solve real user problems.

## Community Presence
- **Username**: Known as "kepano" in forums and discussions.
- **Communication Style**: Often participates in technical discussions and philosophy debates.
- **Influence**: Significant impact on Obsidian's development direction through user feedback.
