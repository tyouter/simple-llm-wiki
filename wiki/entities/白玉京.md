# 白玉京 (Bai Yujing)

## Identity
Zhihu user and the author of the primary, detailed answer in the [[Zhihu Discussion: Optimizing Claude Code with CLAUDE.md and Efficient Development Workflows]]. Holds a Master's degree in Computer Technology from the Communication University of China.

## Contribution and Perspective
白玉京 provided a comprehensive, configuration-heavy guide to optimizing [[Claude Code]]. Their answer represents the **"comprehensive configuration"** philosophy within the discussion.

### Key Arguments and Instructions:
1.  **Advocates for Detailed [[CLAUDE.md]]**: Recommends creating extensive CLAUDE.md files (up to 200 lines) that exhaustively cover project rules, architecture, coding standards, and personal preferences to leave no ambiguity for the AI.
2.  **Emphasis on System Mastery**: Provides step-by-step guidance on configuring Claude Code's [[Persistent Memory System (Claude Code)]], including the use of `.claude/rules/` directories and leveraging [[Auto Memory]].
3.  **Detailed Permission Configuration**: Explains the strategic use of [[Permission Modes (Claude Code)]] (default, plan, auto, bypassPermissions) to streamline different phases of work.
4.  **Structured Workflow**: Suggests a workflow that begins with a `/plan` command, reflecting an implicit endorsement of [[Spec Coding vs. Vibe Coding|Spec Coding]] principles, though embedded within a complex configuration.

## Philosophical Stance
白玉京's approach is rooted in the belief that maximum efficiency from an AI assistant is achieved through meticulous, upfront configuration and instruction. This creates a highly customized and predictable environment for Claude to operate within.

## Relation to Other Contributors
- **Contradiction with [[yahah]]**: Their detailed guide directly contradicts yahah's minimalist philosophy, forming the core debate of the discussion.
- **Contrast with [[笙囧同学]]**: While both invested time in CLAUDE.md, 笙囧同学's conclusion favored brevity, whereas 白玉京's guide exemplifies the detailed approach.
