# Zhihu (知乎)

## Overview
Zhihu is a major Chinese question-and-answer website and knowledge-sharing platform, often compared to Quora. It hosts discussions on a vast array of topics, from professional expertise to everyday life, with an emphasis on in-depth, high-quality answers.

## Role as a Knowledge Repository
Beyond simple Q&A, Zhihu threads often evolve into curated repositories of information and resources. Discussions like [[Zhihu Discussion: Alternative Websites After Tianya Forum Closure]] and [[Zhihu Discussion: Career Transition to Strategic Management and Consulting]] demonstrate how the platform functions as a crowdsourced knowledge base, where users compile and vet valuable information on specific themes.

## Context for the Discussion
As a platform that succeeded the forum era, Zhihu itself is part of the [[Post-Forum Internet Landscape]]. However, its format (topic-based Q&A) differs from the free-flowing, board-style discussion of classic forums like [[Tianya Forum (天涯论坛)]]. The discussed thread on Zhihu about website alternatives is a meta-example of the community using one modern platform to navigate the broader, fragmented ecosystem.
