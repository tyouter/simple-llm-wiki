# Zhihu

## Overview
Zhihu (知乎) is a major Chinese question-and-answer website and online community, often described as the "Chinese Quora." It serves as a platform for knowledge sharing, discussion, and networking, where users can pose questions, provide answers, publish articles, and engage in debates across a wide range of topics.

## Key Characteristics
1.  **User Base**: Comprises a broad spectrum of users, from industry professionals and academics to students and general enthusiasts, contributing to a diverse content ecosystem.
2.  **Content Scope**: Covers professional fields (e.g., technology, business, academia), lifestyle topics, current events, and personal development.
3.  **Platform Mechanics**: Features voting systems (upvotes/downvotes), commenting, content collection, and algorithmic feeds to surface popular and personalized content.
4.  **Monetization & Features**: Includes premium content subscriptions, live sessions, paid consultations, and recruitment services (as indicated by the `jobs@zhihu.com` address on its [[Zhihu Login Page Error Message (Source)]]).
