# 吴恩达 (Andrew Ng)

## Overview
吴恩达 is a renowned AI researcher, co-founder of Coursera, and adjunct professor at Stanford University. He is a leading figure in machine learning education and online learning platforms.

## Relevance to Knowledge Management
He is mentioned in the [[Zhihu]] in two key contexts:

1.  **As a Symbol of Structured Online Learning:** His work with Coursera represents the type of high-quality, structured educational content the guide promotes—moving beyond fragmented information to systematic courses.
2.  **Educational Philosophy:** His signature encouragement, **"Keep learning!"**, embodies the mindset of continuous, self-directed education that the Zhihu answer advocates for.

His presence in the discussion connects the guide's principles to the mainstream online education movement, suggesting that even within platform-based learning, one should seek out the most authoritative instructors and well-structured curricula.

## Connection to Concepts
Ng's work provides a bridge between formal education and self-directed learning. Platforms like Coursera can be components of a [[Self-Directed Learning Infrastructure]], especially when courses are selected based on the instructor's authority and the curriculum's rigor, aligning with [[Information Source Curation]] practices.
