# Brian Chesky

## Description
Co-founder and CEO of Airbnb. He is referenced in the context of **[[GStack]]** as the inspiration for a key philosophical principle within the workflow.

## Influence on AI Workflow Design
Chesky is known for his intense focus on product vision and crafting exceptional, "11-star" user experiences. This philosophy is directly embedded in the GStack command `/plan-ceo-review`. When using this command, the AI is prompted to act as a CEO and define what a "10-star experience" would be for the user, forcing a deep consideration of vision and desire before any technical implementation begins.

His approach exemplifies the high-level, human-centric planning that the GStack workflow seeks to emulate and institutionalize in AI development, countering the AI's tendency to jump straight to code.

**Source:** Identified in the analysis as the inspiration for the `/plan-ceo-review` command's focus.

**Brian Chesky** 是Airbnb的联合创始人兼首席执行官，以其对极致用户体验和产品细节的执着追求而闻名。他提出了“10星体验”的理念，即思考如何让产品或服务好到用户愿意主动向他人推荐。

在[[gstack：Claude工程团队 - 汇智网分析]]的语境中，[[GStack]]项目中的`/plan-ceo-review`技能所体现的思维模式被命名为**[[Brian Chesky模式 (Brian Chesky Mode)]]**。该模式强制AI在规划阶段从产品卓越性和用户狂喜的角度进行根本性质疑，而不是简单地接受和执行初始需求描述。

通过将这种产品思维模式编码进AI技能，[[基于角色的AI开发 (Role-Based AI Development)]]范式将顶尖产品领导者的决策框架工具化，使得开发团队能够在编写代码前，系统性地对齐对“卓越”的愿景，从而从源头提升产品的成功概率。
