# IBM

## Role in Strategic Planning Context
Technology company mentioned as an early adopter and developer of the [[BLM (Business Leadership Model)]] strategic planning methodology.

## BLM Model Development
### Original Development
- IBM played key role in developing the Business Leadership Model
- Created as comprehensive strategic planning and execution framework
- Integrated approach connecting strategy formulation with implementation

### Model Adoption
- Used internally for IBM's strategic management
- Became reference implementation for methodology
- Demonstrated practical effectiveness in large technology organization

## Model Components (IBM Implementation)
### Strategy Formulation
- Market insight driven by technology trends
- Innovation focus aligned with R&D capabilities
- Business design for global technology services

### Strategy Execution
- Formal organization for multinational operations
- Talent development for technical and consulting roles
- Culture supporting innovation and client focus

## Influence on Other Companies
### Methodology Transfer
- BLM methodology adopted by other organizations
- [[华为 (Huawei)]] as notable Chinese adopter
- Adapted to different industries and cultural contexts

### Best Practices Sharing
- Framework structure and implementation approach
- Integration of leadership development with strategy
- Market-driven planning principles
