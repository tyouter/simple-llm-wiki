# TurboDocx

## Description
A team or company that developed a conceptual model for AI-assisted development using a "Director/Manager/Team" role structure. This model is cited as an independent example of **[[Convergent Evolution (in AI Tools)]]** with **[[GStack]]**'s CEO/Engineer/QA model.

## Significance
TurboDocx's work serves as external validation for the core principle of **[[Role-Based AI Development]]**. The fact that two independent groups arrived at a similar hierarchical, role-separated architecture to manage AI interactions suggests it is a robust solution to the fundamental **[[Single-Mode Problem]]** in AI coding assistants.

Their model, while potentially differing in specific roles or implementation, shares the same philosophical foundation as GStack: assigning different cognitive tasks (planning, delegation, execution) to distinct AI personas to improve outcome quality and process control.

**Source:** Cited in the analysis as a comparable example to GStack.
