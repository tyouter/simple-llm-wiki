# Tianya Forum (天涯论坛)

## Overview
Tianya Forum was a highly influential and iconic Chinese online forum and social platform. For years, it served as a major hub for user-generated content, including personal stories, serialized novels, news discussions, and social commentary, forming a unique digital culture and collective memory for a generation of Chinese netizens.

## Cultural Significance
Often described as a "corner of the Chinese internet," Tianya was known for its vibrant, sometimes raw, community discourse. It was a place where grassroots narratives could gain national attention and where many internet memes and trends originated before spreading to other platforms.

## Closure & Legacy
Its closure marked the end of an era for the Chinese internet, symbolizing the shift away from large, open, text-based forums.
