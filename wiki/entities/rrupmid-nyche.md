# Rrupmid Nyche

## Description
An AI + Art PhD student and creative practitioner who enjoys building custom tools and exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, design, and code. They actively document and share workflows, tips, and insights related to AI-assisted design and development.

## Documented Workflow
[[Rrupmid Nyche]] is the author of the source documentation describing the method for [[Using DesignPrompts.dev to Generate High-Quality AI-Free Interfaces with Claude Code]]. In this work, they share a practical, step-by-step process for leveraging curated prompts to achieve unique, non-generic interface designs with AI assistance.

## Interests & Contributions
- Developing methods to avoid stereotypical "AI aesthetics" in generated work.
- Exploring the use of niche tools and prompt repositories like [[designprompts.dev]].
- Experimenting with [[AI Tool Chaining/Specialization for Design]] and [[Prompt Combination & Adaptation]] techniques.
- Advocating for creative and intentional use of AI in the design process, moving beyond generic outputs.
