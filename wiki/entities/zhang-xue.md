# Zhang Xue (张雪)

## Identity
Chinese motorcycle racer, engineer, and entrepreneur. Founder of [[Zhang Xue Motorcycle]]. Rose from poverty as a mechanic to become a world championship-winning motorcycle manufacturer.

## Early Life and Background
- Came from impoverished background with limited formal education
- Began as a motorcycle mechanic, developing deep technical understanding through hands-on work
- Initially pursued dream of becoming professional motorcycle racer

## Career Transformation

### Racing Aspiration
Initially focused on becoming a competitive racer, but faced limitations in reaching world championship level as a rider.

### Pivotal Mentorship
Met champion racer [[Zhang Jixing]] who recognized his technical passion over raw racing talent and advised the [[Strategic Pivot from Racing to Manufacturing]].

### Media Breakthrough
Through [[Perseverance Performance]] (chasing reporter [[Yi Jun]] for 100km in hailstorm), secured documentary coverage that launched public recognition.

## Entrepreneurial Journey

### First Venture: Kaiyue
Founded initial motorcycle company Kaiyue (凯越), which later won a WSBK championship using technical foundations he established.

### Second Venture: Zhang Xue Motorcycle
After leaving Kaiyue, founded [[Zhang Xue Motorcycle]] with support from [[Zhang Jixing]].

### Investment Success
Secured 90 million RMB investment from state-owned venture capital firm [[Zhechuangtou]], based largely on confidence in his character and technical能力.

## Key Characteristics

### Technical Expertise
Deep, practical understanding of motorcycle engineering developed through hands-on mechanical work rather than formal education.

### Authenticity
Exemplifies [[Authenticity as Capital]] - genuine passion and straightforward character that built trust with mentors, media, and investors.

### Perseverance
Demonstrated extreme dedication through actions like the 100km chase, embodying [[Perseverance Performance]].

### Dream-Driven
Personifies [[Dream-First, Business-Second Entrepreneurship]], with business structure serving his passion for building championship motorcycles.

## Achievements
- Founded two successful motorcycle companies
- Won WSBK (World Superbike Championship) world championship in 2026 with [[Zhang Xue Motorcycle]]
- Created acclaimed models including ZX-500RR and 820RR
- Transformed from impoverished mechanic to industry-recognized entrepreneur

## Philosophical Approach
Believes in building motorcycles that "can become world champions" rather than personally becoming champion racer, implementing the [[Strategic Pivot from Racing to Manufacturing]] advised by his mentor.
