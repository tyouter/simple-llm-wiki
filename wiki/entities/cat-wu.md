# Cat Wu

## Role
Product Manager at [[Anthropic]]'s Research PM team.

## Background
- Formerly product engineer at Scale AI and Dagster
- Previously worked as a venture capital analyst
- Author of the blog post detailing AI-driven product management transformation at Anthropic

## Contributions
### AI-Driven Product Management Transformation
Cat Wu authored a detailed account of how exponential AI model improvements have fundamentally disrupted traditional product management assumptions. Her analysis describes:

#### New Workflow Patterns
- Using [[Claude.ai]] for strategy development and problem-solving
- Using [[Claude Code]] for prototyping and development
- Using [[Cowork]] for administrative tasks
- This represents a practical implementation of [[AI Tool Chaining/Combination]]

#### Organizational Shifts
Wu identifies four key shifts at Anthropic:
1. From long-term planning to rapid experimentation
2. From documentation-first to [[Prototype-First Development]]
3. From complex workarounds to following the [[Simplest Working Solution Principle]]
4. From static features to [[Model-Capability-Driven Product Evolution]]

#### Conceptual Framework
Her work contributes to the development of [[AI-Exponential Product Management]] as a new paradigm that accounts for rapidly improving AI capabilities.

## Professional Perspective
Wu's background spanning product engineering, venture capital, and now AI product management gives her a unique perspective on:
- The practical implications of exponential AI improvement
- Organizational adaptation to technological change
- Balancing innovation with practical implementation

## Impact
Her analysis has been referenced by other product leaders including:
- [[Bihan Jiang]] who reports that Claude significantly increases the ceiling for high-quality product teams
- [[Kai Xin Tai]] who notes the shift from locking in certainty to accelerating discovery

Based on her blog post analyzing product management transformation at Anthropic.
