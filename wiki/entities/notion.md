## 概述
一款一体化的笔记、知识库、项目和数据库管理工具，以其高度的灵活性和可定制性而受到广泛欢迎。

## 在本框架中的角色
在[[AI决策五步法：提升判断与决策能力的结构化框架]]中，**Notion** 扮演着**决策系统的“承载平台”和“迭代引擎”** 的角色。它主要在两个层面发挥作用：
1.  **[[评估层（决策第四步）]]的核心工具**：利用其数据库（Database）和属性（Property）功能，可以轻松构建一个结构化的决策评分系统。决策者可以为每个选项创建页面，设置评分维度（属性），并灵活调整权重，实现类似[[Balanced Scorecard|平衡计分卡]]的量化分析。
2.  **[[决策系统迭代]]的实现基础**：由于Notion中的所有逻辑和数据都被持久化保存，整个评分模型和决策框架得以沉淀下来。当需要重新评估或遇到类似决策时，可以直接复制模板、修改参数，实现决策系统的快速复用和持续优化。这使得决策从一次性的项目，变成了可积累、可迭代的组织资产。

**Notion** is an all-in-one workspace application that popularized the block-based editor paradigm in mainstream productivity software. Its intuitive drag-and-drop interface for rearranging content blocks—text, lists, databases, media—has set user expectations for fluid content manipulation across the software industry.

Notion's block model exemplifies the [[Text Block as a Unit of Manipulation]] concept at scale, treating all content as modular, movable units. This paradigm has been so successful that it's frequently borrowed by other applications through [[Paradigm Borrowing in UX]], as seen in the [[Obsidian Dragger]] plugin's explicit goal to bring "Notion-like" functionality to [[Obsidian]].

While Notion offers a more polished, integrated experience out-of-the-box compared to Obsidian's extensible-but-requires-assembly approach, it represents a different philosophical stance: cloud-based, proprietary, and opinionated versus local, open, and customizable. The tension between these approaches drives innovation in both camps, with each borrowing successful elements from the other while maintaining core identities.
