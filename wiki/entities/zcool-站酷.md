# ZCOOL (站酷)

## Overview
ZCOOL is a major Chinese interactive platform and community for designers, illustrators, photographers, and other visual artists. It functions as a portfolio site, social network, and inspiration hub for the creative industry.

## As a Niche Creative Hub
ZCOOL is a definitive example of a [[Niche Knowledge & Creative Hubs|Creative Hub]]. It provides a focused space for professionals and enthusiasts to share work, gather feedback, find inspiration, and connect with clients or collaborators. Its mention in the [[Zhihu Discussion: Alternative Websites After Tianya Forum Closure]] underscores its status as a go-to, high-quality destination for a specific creative community.

## Contrast with General Forums
Unlike a general forum like the former [[Tianya Forum (天涯论坛)]], ZCOOL's design, features, and community norms are all tailored to the specific needs of visual creators. This specialization is a key characteristic of the [[Post-Forum Internet Landscape]], where distinct professional and hobbyist communities congregate on platforms built for their specific modes of interaction and content sharing.
