# 豆包 (Doubao)

## Overview
ByteDance's AI model mentioned in the DeepSeek analysis as having become overly cautious ('豆书记') in responses compared to [[DeepSeek]], illustrating differences in response styles among Chinese AI models.

## Response Characteristics

### Cautious Approach
- Tendency toward conservative and safe responses
- Avoidance of potentially controversial topics
- Careful wording and qualification in answers

### Comparison with [[DeepSeek]]
- DeepSeek provides more direct and useful answers
- Different balance between capability and caution   - Varied willingness to engage with complex queries

## Development Context

### Developer: ByteDance
- Major Chinese technology company   - Experience with content platforms and moderation   - Different priorities and constraints than [[幻方量化 (Huanfang Quantitative)]]

### Market Position
