# Jeff Bezos

## Description
Founder and former CEO of Amazon, widely regarded as one of the most influential business leaders and innovators of the internet era.

## Relevance to the Discussion
In the [[Zhihu Discussion: The Role of 'Scenarios' in Product Management]], a contributor references a famous principle articulated by Bezos to support long-term, stable product thinking that is relevant to scenario analysis.

## Key Principle Referenced
- **"Focus on what won't change"**: Bezos has often stated that at Amazon, they focus on things they know will be important to customers ten years from now (e.g., lower prices, faster delivery, vast selection), rather than chasing what might change. This provides stability and direction for long-term investment.

## Application to Scenario Analysis
The contributor applied this principle to the practice of [[Scenario Analysis (场景分析)]]:
- While specific technologies and interfaces may change, the fundamental human needs and goals within certain scenarios are enduring.
- For example, the need for "convenient nourishment during a short work break" is stable, even if the solutions evolve from vending machines to food delivery apps.
- By identifying these stable, enduring needs within user scenarios, product teams can build with long-term value in mind, avoiding the trap of designing for transient trends or technologies.

This reference connects the tactical tool of scenario analysis to a strategic, long-term business philosophy.
