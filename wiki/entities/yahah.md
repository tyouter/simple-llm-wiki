# yahah

## Identity
Zhihu user and the author of the contrasting, minimalist answer in the [[Zhihu Discussion: Optimizing Claude Code with CLAUDE.md and Efficient Development Workflows]]. A PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

## Contribution and Perspective
yahah provided a concise, methodology-focused counterpoint to the detailed configuration guide. Their answer represents the **"minimalist, spec-driven"** philosophy.

### Key Arguments and Instructions:
1.  **Advocates for Extreme Brevity in [[CLAUDE.md]]**: Provides a very concise CLAUDE.md template (~50 lines). Argues that the file should only contain essential project context and a strict, clear prohibition against [[Vibe Coding]].
2.  **Shifts Focus to Workflow**: Contends that over-reliance on complex internal AI configuration is less critical than employing disciplined external development methodologies.
3.  **Promotes [[Spec Coding vs. Vibe Coding|Spec Coding]]**: Strongly advocates for writing detailed specifications before any implementation as the core of effective AI collaboration.
4.  **References Advanced Workflows**: Mentions the [[Humanize Workflow]] (multi-agent review) and SWE-agent driven development as examples of robust, external processes that matter more than internal Claude settings.
5.  **Exemplifies [[AI Tool Chaining/Combination]]**: Suggests using Claude alongside other tools/agents like Codex, viewing the AI as part of a toolchain.

## Philosophical Stance
yahah's approach is rooted in software engineering principles: clear requirements (specs), defined processes, and tool composition. They believe the developer's discipline and the chosen high-level workflow are primary; the AI assistant's configuration is secondary and should be simple.

## Relation to Other Contributors
- **Core Contradiction with [[白玉京]]**: Their minimalist template and workflow emphasis directly oppose 白玉京's comprehensive configuration guide. This is the central contradiction of the source discussion.
- **Alignment with [[笙囧同学]]**: Both arrived at the conclusion that shorter CLAUDE.md files are more effective, though yahah's argument is more principled and methodological.

# yahah

## 身份
知乎答主，中国科学院大学计算机科学与技术博士在读。在[[Claude Code高效配置与开发实践指南]]的讨论中，提供了高度凝练、哲学化的技术见解和工程实践。

## 核心贡献
### 方法论深度探讨
- **[[Spec Coding]]哲学**：系统阐述了反对[[Vibe Coding]]的严谨工程方法，强调规格说明和计划的重要性
- **[[Humanize]]模式**：详细描述了利用迭代反馈循环的协同开发模式
- **[[RLCR]]方法论**：介绍了针对大规模代码修改的系统化方法，分享了gem5项目成功案例

### 配置哲学
提供了极其精炼的[[CLAUDE.md]]示例，代表了"核心共识派"的配置哲学。与[[白玉京]]的"详尽规范派"形成对比，强调极致简洁而非详尽罗列。

## 技术观点
### 反对Vibe Coding
明确批判依赖模糊感觉的[[Vibe Coding]]，提倡更具工程性的[[Spec Coding]]。认为Vibe Coding本质是将自然语言直接"投影"为编程语言，缺乏中间的设计规划环节。

### 强调计划驱动
在[[Spec Coding]]框架中，特别强调[[Plan]]的重要性。认为Plan应该详细到"按照这个步骤，一个本科生也能在一个月内完成"，大型项目的Plan可能长达5-10页A4纸。

### 大规模工程实践
分享了[[RLCR]]方法论的实际应用经验，特别是"蜂群+RLCR"处理10k-30k行代码改动的策略，以及使用带`/batch`命令的RLCR处理超过3万行的大规模改动。

## 成功案例
**gem5构建系统迁移**：使用RLCR结合"蜂群"在四小时内完成了gem5仓库（计算机系统架构模拟器）构建系统从SCons到CMake的彻底替换。这个案例展示了高级方法论在实际大型工程中的威力。

## 相关概念
- [[Spec Coding]] - 主要倡导的方法论
- [[Vibe Coding]] - 明确反对的方法
- [[Plan]] - 强调的核心组件
- [[Humanize]] - 介绍的协同模式
- [[RLCR]] - 分享的大规模修改方法
- gem5 - 成功案例项目

## 影响
提供了从工具使用到工程哲学的升华，将[[Claude Code高效配置与开发实践指南]]的讨论从"如何配置"提升到"如何思考开发范式"的层面。
