# 海伦娜·坦格利安

## Role in Discussion
Answerer in the [[Zhihu Discussion: Comparative Analysis of Foreign vs. Domestic Textbooks]] who provides a highly memorable, succinct, and satirical framework for comparing textbook philosophies.

## Key Contribution: The Tripartite Model
Formulated a widely cited comparison of textbook styles based on their underlying assumptions about the learner:
1. **American**: "Teach to the 'fool'." (Scaffolded, accessible)
2. **Soviet**: "Teach to the 'genius'." (Rigorous, abstract)
3. **Contemporary Chinese (Critique)**: "Teach the 'fool' as if they are a 'genius'." (Perceived as combining the difficulty of the second with the learner profile of the first)

## Impact
This pithy summary crystallized the core [[Pedagogical Approach Comparison]] at the heart of the discussion. It provided a shared vocabulary and conceptual framework for other users to articulate their critiques and experiences.

## Style
Uses satire and exaggeration to make a pointed critique of perceived flaws in some modern domestic educational materials, while also acknowledging the distinct philosophies behind American and historical Soviet approaches.
