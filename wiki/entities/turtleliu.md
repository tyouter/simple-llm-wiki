## The KMP Algorithm
While not explained in detail in their brief contribution, the KMP algorithm is notable for:
- Precomputing a "partial match" table from the search pattern
- Achieving O(n+m) time complexity for searching
- Eliminating the need to backtrack in the text being searched
- Being both theoretically optimal and practically useful
