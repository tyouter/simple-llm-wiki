# Khan Academy (可汗学院)

## Overview
A non-profit educational organization and free online learning platform that provides practice exercises, instructional videos, and a personalized learning dashboard across a wide range of subjects.

## Role in the Discussion
In the [[Zhihu Discussion: Comparative Analysis of Foreign vs. Domestic Textbooks]], [[知乎用户]] specifically recommends Khan Academy as a premier example of [[Open Educational Resources (OER)]] and effective digital pedagogy.

## Key Praise
- **Self-Study Friendly Design**: The platform's structure—short video lessons, interactive practice, and immediate feedback—exemplifies the principles of [[Self-Study Friendly Textbook Design]] in a digital format.
- **Subject Highlight**: The user notes its particular strength and usefulness for **humanities subjects**, where its explanatory videos can effectively unpack complex concepts in history, art, and civics.
- **Accessibility**: As a free resource, it addresses cost barriers and serves as a practical tool for self-learners worldwide.

## Connection to Broader Themes
Khan Academy represents the evolution of the textbook into an interactive, adaptive learning system. It is a direct embodiment of the movement toward accessible, learner-centric education discussed in the thread, related to the work of other online education pioneers like [[吴恩达]].
