# Token侦探

## Background
An independent AI researcher and the primary answer author in the Zhihu discussion [[Zhihu Discussion: The Era of Super Individuals and Agent Shock]].

## Contribution & Perspective
Provided a comprehensive, data-driven, and economic analysis of the impending transformation driven by AI Agents. His answer is structured as a forecast of systemic disruption.

## Key Arguments Presented
1.  **Framed the "Super Individual" Concept**: Defined it through the formula `1 person + N Agents = 1 company's output` and positioned it as an economic inevitability.
2.  **Coined "Agent Shock"**: Drew a direct and powerful analogy between the impact of AI on services and the "China Shock" on manufacturing, citing research by economist [[David Autor]].
3.  **Analyzed Macro Trends**: Identified [[Service Trade Globalization]] as the next phase of globalization, enabled by AI Agents removing traditional frictions.
4.  **Predicted Market Dynamics**: Argued for a "winner-takes-all" outcome in globalized service markets, using examples like [[MrBeast]] on digital platforms as a precursor.

## Analytical Style
His methodology is framework-driven and economic in nature, similar in rigor to strategic frameworks found in other Zhihu discussions (e.g., [[7-Step Strategic Planning Framework]]), though applied to a futuristic, disruptive scenario.

## Role in the Discussion
Serves as the primary theorist and forecaster, providing the overarching economic narrative that contextualizes the more personal, operational advice from other contributors like [[丹爷OS]].
