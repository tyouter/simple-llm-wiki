# Marty Cagan

## Description
A highly influential author, speaker, and thought leader in the field of product management. He is the founder of the Silicon Valley Product Group (SVPG) and author of the seminal book *Inspired: How to Create Tech Products Customers Love* (published in Chinese as *启示录*).

## Relevance to the Discussion
In the [[Zhihu Discussion: The Role of 'Scenarios' in Product Management]], contributors reference Cagan's work to support the argument for deep user understanding over feature-focused development. His philosophy is a foundational influence on modern product management best practices.

## Key Ideas Referenced
- **Outcome over Output**: The primary focus should be on the value and outcome delivered to the customer and the business, not merely on shipping features (output).
- **Deep Customer Understanding**: Product teams must gain a profound understanding of their customers' needs, pains, and contexts, which aligns directly with the practice of [[Scenario Analysis (场景分析)]].
- **Contrast with "Feature Teams"**: He criticizes teams that are organized around delivering a roadmap of predefined features without continuous discovery and validation with users.

His emphasis on discovering and understanding the complete "user story"—why users behave the way they do and what they truly need—provides a theoretical backbone for the practical scenario analysis methods discussed on Zhihu.
