# Google Stitch AI Design Platform Update and Market Impact

## Summary
Google's Stitch AI design platform has been repositioned as a 'vibe design platform' with natural language-to-interface capabilities, causing Figma's stock to drop 8.8%. The article details Stitch's features, provides hands-on testing, and discusses polarized reactions about its potential to disrupt traditional UI/UX workflows and the design industry.

## Key Details
- **Platform:** [[Google Stitch]] (Google Labs)
- **Core Technology:** Powered by [[Gemini]] AI models (Gemini 3.0 Flash for speed, Gemini 3.1 Pro for quality)
- **Market Impact:** [[Figma]] stock dropped 8.8% following announcement, with cumulative decline of ~80% since August 2025 IPO
- **Expert Analysis:** [[Ethan Mollick]] provided measured assessment, noting Stitch applies 'vibework' beyond coding to design
