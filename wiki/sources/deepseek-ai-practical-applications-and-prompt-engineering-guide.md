## Key Findings

### DeepSeek's Competitive Advantages
- **Superior Chinese Language Understanding**: Outperforms [[GPT]] and other Chinese models in Chinese text generation and cultural context comprehension

- **Less Restrictive Responses**: Compared to [[豆包 (Doubao)]] which has become overly cautious ('豆书记'), DeepSeek provides more direct and useful answers
