### Security-First Mindset
The discussion strongly emphasizes security risks, with [[糖炒栗子]] providing a 15-point safety checklist. Core recommendations include:
- **Mandatory Isolation**: Deploying OpenClaw exclusively in [[虚拟机隔离部署 (Virtual Machine Isolation Deployment)]] environments
- **Permission Minimization**: Running with minimal system permissions
- **Skill Vetting**: Careful evaluation of third-party skills due to [[技能安装风险 (Skill Installation Risks)]]
