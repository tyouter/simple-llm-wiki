# Zhihu Discussion: Alternative Websites After Tianya Forum Closure

## Summary
A discussion thread on [[Zhihu (知乎)]] where users share and recommend niche, high-quality websites they frequent following the closure of the iconic [[Tianya Forum (天涯论坛)]]. The thread serves as a curated repository of over 30 specialized sites, reflecting a broader shift in the Chinese internet landscape from monolithic, general-purpose forums to a distributed ecosystem of interest-based communities.

## Key Themes & Curation
In response to the question "What are some niche, high-quality websites you often visit?", the answers collectively map a [[Post-Forum Internet Landscape]]. The recommendations are not algorithmically generated but represent [[Curated Digital Exploration]]—personal endorsements from knowledgeable users. The sites are categorized into several domains:
- **Digital Archives & Knowledge**: Sites like [[Shuge.org (书格)]] (ancient books) and the digital portal of the [[China National Library (中国国家图书馆)]].
- **Creative & Design Hubs**: Platforms such as [[ZCOOL (站酷)]] for designers and artists.
- **Learning & Skill Development**: Websites offering tutorials, courses, and practical knowledge.
- **Entertainment & Culture**: Niche forums and sites for specific hobbies, media, and cultural discussion.
- **Tools & Utilities**: Practical websites for specific tasks.

## Significance of the Discussion
This Zhihu thread is more than a simple list; it is a crowdsourced response to a cultural moment—the end of a major centralized forum. It demonstrates how users are navigating the fragmented modern web by building and sharing personal directories of [[Niche Knowledge & Creative Hubs]]. The discussion itself becomes a valuable, human-curated resource, similar in function to the existing wiki entry [[Zhihu Discussion: Career Transition to Strategic Management and Consulting]].
